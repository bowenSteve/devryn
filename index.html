About us

Devryn Limited is a Kenyan company Incorporated under the Companies Act, 2015. The company is dully registered and authorized to offers Engineering Construction and Construction Project management services to individuals and organizations.The Company work closely with clients to accurately interpret their dreams, visions in drawings and bring them to the desired reality through construction solutions to meet their needs; We communicate with our clients throughout the construction process to keep them informed of progress and to ensure that our project keeps on schedule and within the clients budget
